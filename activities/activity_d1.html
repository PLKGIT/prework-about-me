Bonus

Make a sandwich

  1. Enter kitchen
  2. Open food cabinet
  3. Pull bread from first shelf
  4. Pull peanut butter from second shelf
  5. Close cabinet
  6. Open utensils drawer
  7. Pull knife from drawer
  8. Close utensils drawer
  9. Open bread bag and remove two (2) slices of bread
  10. Close bread bag
  11. Unscrew peanut butter jar
  12. Pull peanut butter from jar with knife
  13. Close peanut butter jar
  14. Spread peanut butter on bread with knife
  15. Place slices of bread together, peanut butter inside
  16. Enjoy your sandwich!

    17. P.S. Don't forget to put everything away.
